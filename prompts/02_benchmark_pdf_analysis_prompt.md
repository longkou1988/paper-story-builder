# 02 Benchmark PDF Analysis Prompt

Task: analyze the user-provided benchmark article PDF for structure only.

Output `output/benchmark_structure_analysis.md` with:

- article title, journal, year, and article type when available
- section order
- Introduction paragraph count and function sequence
- theory section organization
- hypothesis development organization
- method/results/discussion section placement
- contribution placement
- limitations/future research style
- overall story pattern

Mandatory boundary sentence:

`paper-story-builder 只学习对标论文的结构和论证范式，不复制其文本表达。`

Do not copy, translate, or closely paraphrase benchmark text. Do not import benchmark variables, theories, sample, findings, or contribution into the user's study.
