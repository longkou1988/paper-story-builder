# 03 Benchmark Rhetorical Moves Prompt

Task: extract paragraph-level rhetorical moves from the benchmark article.

Focus on function, not wording:

- practical phenomenon
- prior research stream
- theoretical gap
- research question
- theoretical lens
- model/design preview
- contribution claim
- transition

Output `output/benchmark_rhetorical_moves.md` with paragraph numbers, section names, rhetorical function, and transferable structural lesson.

Avoid long quotations. If quoting is necessary for location evidence, use only a minimal phrase and clearly label it as benchmark evidence.
