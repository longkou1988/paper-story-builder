# 04 Story Chain Building Prompt

Task: build `output/story_chain.md` from the user's own topic and model evidence.

Include:

1. research phenomenon
2. managerial problem
3. prior research progress
4. research limitations
5. theory gap
6. entry point
7. core research question
8. theoretical lens
9. model structure
10. variable relationships
11. hypothesis logic
12. theoretical contribution
13. practical contribution
14. mapping to benchmark structure
15. reviewer risks

Rules:

- Do not regenerate topics.
- Do not redesign the model.
- Do not add unapproved variables or paths.
- Do not make a variable collage.
- Mark unsupported claims as `needs additional literature support`.
