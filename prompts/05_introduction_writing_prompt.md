# 05 Introduction Writing Prompt

Task: create `output/introduction_outline.md` and `output/introduction_draft.md`.

Use a management SSCI funnel structure:

1. broad phenomenon
2. specific managerial problem
3. prior research stream
4. unresolved theoretical gap
5. research question
6. theoretical lens
7. model and design preview
8. contributions
9. paper roadmap

Rules:

- English by default unless the user requests Chinese.
- Every important claim must be grounded in prior outputs, user notes, or marked `needs additional literature support`.
- Do not claim novelty such as "first study" without explicit evidence.
- Do not reuse benchmark text.
