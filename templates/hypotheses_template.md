## Quality Checks
- No hypothesis may introduce variables absent from the model specification unless clearly marked as a proposed extension.
- Moderation hypotheses must describe the moderated path, not a direct effect on the outcome.
- Any unsupported causal language must be softened or marked for additional literature support.
